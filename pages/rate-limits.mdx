# Limits on RapidAPI

ApiMask request limits, quotas, and throttling are controlled by the RapidAPI product
and plan you subscribe to.

## Where to check limits

Use your RapidAPI dashboard to view:

- Current plan
- Monthly request quota
- Remaining usage
- Rate-limit responses
- Upgrade options

## Common limit response

When a plan limit is reached, RapidAPI may return `429 Too Many Requests`. Upgrade or
wait for the quota window to reset according to the plan details shown on RapidAPI.
